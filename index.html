About Us

Our teachers have 20 years experience teaching all age groups in Ballet, Jazz/Modern Dance and Tap.

Originally based in Dublin we relocated to Belfast 5 years ago and have thoroughly enjoyed the new challenges that the relocation brought.

Certification

Our teachers are qualified with both the Royal Academy of Dance and the International Dance Teachers Association.

Our examiners are Royal Academy of Dance accredited and we teach grades 1 - 10.

Child Safety

We are fully vetted to work with children and vulnerable people.

Our teachers own children have been students since they could walk and have furthered their studies at third level.
